About Us

Empowering Global Trade with Innovative Solutions

Your Trusted Partner for International Trade, Import-Export Services, and Business Consulting

MMK Consultants is a leading provider of international trade and consulting solutions, specializing in import, export, and general trading. Founded with a vision to bridge global markets, we cater to diverse industries such as FMCG, metallurgy, plastics, chemicals, and construction materials. With headquarters in Concord, Ontario, Canada, and strategical offices in the UAE and Bangladesh, we connect buyers and sellers across Asia, Africa, and the Americas. Our Story

The Story of MMK Group

A Vision Transformed into a Global Empire

The journey of MMK Group is not just about business; it's about passion, perseverance, and purpose. Founded by Mr. Mohd Mehrab Musa in 2008, MMK Group has evolved from a small entrepreneurial venture into a global conglomerate, driven by a singular vision: to build sustainable, impactful businesses that make a difference. From humble beginnings, MMK Group has grown by embracing innovation, challenging the status quo, and striving for excellence across multiple industries. Today, MMK Group stands as a testament to what can be achieved when vision meets determination.
