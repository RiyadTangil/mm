Our Story

The Story of MMK Group

A Vision Transformed into a Global Empire

View Timeline

The journey of MMK Group is not just about business; it's about passion, perseverance, and purpose. Founded by Mr. Mohd Mehrab Musa in 2008, MMK Group has evolved from a small entrepreneurial venture into a global conglomerate, driven by a singular vision: to build sustainable, impactful businesses that make a difference. From humble beginnings, MMK Group has grown by embracing innovation, challenging the status quo, and striving for excellence across multiple industries. Today, MMK Group stands as a testament to what can be achieved when vision meets determination.

Timeline

A Timeline of Vision and Growth. The Story of Growth and Excellence

2008
The Foundation of a Dream

Sakura Ultra Flow was born with a mission to tackle the growing water management challenges in Bangladesh. Founded by Mr. Mohd Mehrab Musa, the company quickly became a trusted name in water treatment, working closely with government bodies, the Bangladesh Army, and the Chittagong City Corporation. What began as a solution to a pressing local issue set the stage for the expansive journey ahead.

2010
Shaping the Future of Agriculture

The launch of MMK Agro Management was a pivotal moment in the Group's history. Responding to the increasing demand for high-quality agricultural products, MMK Agro set out to revolutionize the global agricultural trade landscape. By building a vast network of suppliers and producers, the company became a key player in the international market, bringing essential commodities like fruits, vegetables, and grains to countries worldwide.

2013
Weaving a New Future in Textiles

As Bangladesh's garment industry began to rise, Surma Tex was founded in 2013, bringing expertise to the ready-made garment (RMG) sector. Surma Tex was not just another textile company—it was a vision for creating a hub of high-quality sourcing solutions. This division focused on producing knitwear and woven products, building strong relationships with buyers, and ensuring the highest standards of quality. Surma Tex became synonymous with exceptional craftsmanship and global trust.

2015
Taking Trade Global

In 2015, MMK Trading was launched, marking the company's ambitious step into international trading. Initially focused on fresh fruits, vegetables, and seafood, MMK Trading expanded its footprint, creating a seamless trade network that spanned multiple countries and a wide variety of products. This marked a pivotal shift as MMK Group's reach extended beyond borders, laying the foundation for further diversification.

2020
A Year of Bold Expansion

When the world was faced with an unprecedented crisis, MMK Health Services rose to the challenge, providing medical technology and equipment during the COVID-19 pandemic. It was a moment that defined the group's resilience and commitment to society's well-being.

At the same time, MMK Group made a series of strategic moves that would shape its future. MMK Construction was established to meet the growing demand for large-scale infrastructure projects, offering end-to-end solutions with a focus on sustainability and quality.

  • • MMK Consultants was launched to help businesses across industries grow, enhance their brands, and optimize their operations.
  • • MMK Investments brought new opportunities to the table, focusing on cross-border mergers, acquisitions, and investments in renewable energy, particularly solar power plants.
  • • MMK Motivates was founded to guide aspiring entrepreneurs, provide executive coaching, and optimize productivity.
2021
Growth and Global Leadership

MMK Investments strengthened its role as a key player in the world of venture capital, helping early-stage companies grow and supporting cross-border mergers and acquisitions. With a focus on sustainability and innovation, MMK Group's investments continued to flourish.

2022
Leadership in Innovation

MMK Motivates grew into a leading platform for entrepreneurship coaching, offering business owners the tools to create sustainable growth. The group also expanded into new markets and invested in cutting-edge technologies, solidifying its reputation as an innovator and thought leader.

2023 and Beyond
A Vision for the Future

MMK Group's vision is as strong as ever: to continue its journey of global expansion, focusing on technological advancements, sustainability, and making a positive impact. As we look to the future, MMK Group is committed to upholding the values of responsibility, integrity, and community, and we're excited about the opportunities that lie ahead.
